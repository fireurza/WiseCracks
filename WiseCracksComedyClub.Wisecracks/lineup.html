Kraz-Krack

Krystal Wells

Dallas native Krystal Wells captivates the audience from the beginning to end. Her infectious energy will leave you wanting more. Krystal caught her big break at Addison Improv. Krystal blew up the stage and has been doing so ever since.

Draw-Krack

John Little

John is a nationally accredited comedian. He has formerly been seen on BET’s hit show Coming to the Stage, and John will be touring with Nephew Tommy from the Steve Harvey Morning radio show. Look no further for a comic to give you life’s best medicine……Laughter!!!!

draw_krack@laughme.com

Astro-Krack

Aaron Kurth

Aaron is one our top-rated acts which is why he was chosen Best Comedian of 2016! Aaron’s known for hysterical takes on dysfunctional families & workplace insanity. Aaron has performed at all the top comedy clubs.

astro_krack@lauhgme.com

Waz-Krack

Josh Wang

Out of San Jose - An incredible sarcastic individual, Josh Wang has a Richard Pryor like ability to bring his entire life on stage and have everyone relate to him. This, combined with his ability to poke fun at everyday stereotypes allows him to entertain all types of audiences.

waz_krack@laughme.com

EZ-Krack

Eric Baker

This Louisiana native is a brilliant combination of Southern charm and witty edge. His unassuming demeanor masks an intelligent, cynical mind that will have you laugh and think. Forever in the moment, his smile and natural comedic instinct connects with the entire room.

ez_krack@laughme.com
